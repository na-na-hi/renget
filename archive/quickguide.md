Quick Guide

>1. Install Stable Diffusion with Automatic 1111 https://stable-diffusion-art.com/install-windows/
>2. Download a good model off civit.ai. I found one specifically for inpainting clothes.
https://civitai.com/models/116201/inpaint-clothing-editor
Put it in stable-diffusion-webui\models\Stable-diffusion
>3. Go to Img2Img -> Inpaint, make sure you're set to the correct model.

>4.Paste in the original image you want to modify.

>5. Add your positive and negative prompts

>Example positive:
masterpiece, conservative dress, fully clothed. very modest clothing, fully clothed, covered breasts

canvas frame, cartoon, 3d, ((disfigured)), ((bad art)), ((deformed)),((extra limbs)),((close up)),((b&w)), wierd colors, blurry, (((duplicate))), ((morbid)), ((mutilatd), [out of frame], extra fingers, mutated hands, ((poorly drawn hands)), ((poorly drawn face)), (((mutation))), (((deformed))), ((ugly)), blurry, ((bad anatomy)), (((bad proportions))), ((extra limbs)), cloned face, (((disfigured))), out of frame, ugly, extra limbs, (bad anatomy), gross proportions, (malformed limbs), ((missing arms)), ((missing legs)), (((extra arms))), (((extra legs))), mutated hands, (fused fingers), (too many fingers), (((long neck))), Photoshop, video game, ugly, tiling, poorly drawn hands, poorly drawn feet, poorly drawn face, out of frame, mutation, mutated, extra limbs, extra legs, extra arms, disfigured, deformed, cross-eye, body out of frame, blurry, bad art, bad anatomy, 3d render, cleavage, nsfw, sexual, breasts

>6. Paint out the area you want modified

>7. Change batch number to the amount of pics you want generated

>8. Click "Generate"
