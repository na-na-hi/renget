##### ~ ChatGPT: Special Tokens ~

The following special tokens will be interpreted by ChatGPT as empty space, all in lower-case:
```
<|endoftext|>
<|eos|>
<|pad|>
<|bos|>
<|eol|>
<|math|>
<|doc|>
```
ChatGPT will also stop outputting text whenever it says `<|endoftext|>` for example.
