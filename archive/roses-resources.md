#### -> ***tips*** <-

-> *no idea why but I LOVE reading the tips section
in resource sites when I'm bored LOLDOAWK* <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **resource** carrds and rentrys are great for
finding a variety of pixels , though [tumblr](https://www.tumblr.com) can
be more helpful if you're searching for specific
pixels ! ( ex. #pink pixels ; #sanrio pixels ) <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **pinterest** is better when searching for cute
standalone images ( it can be harder to match
[pinterest](https://www.pinterest.com) images without editing them ) ; it's very
useful for finding character art , character chibis ,
etc ! [(example)](https://www.pinterest.ca/search/pins/?q=barbara%20genshin%20chibi&rs) <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **finding** rentry graphics is easiest on tumblr ,
usually with the tag [#rentry graphics](https://www.tumblr.com/search/rentry%20graphics) ! a few posts
include [1](https://www.tumblr.com/selphim/717820161599635456/plutia-from-hyperdimension-neptunia-rentry?source=share) & [2](https://www.tumblr.com/nyamuchis) <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **the tumblr** [lostsozai](https://www.tumblr.com/lostsozai) has many pixels and is
great for browsing to find niche graphics ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **for editing** I personally use [pixlr e](https://pixlr.com/e)
to edit my own rentry graphics since I'm on
a computer ; however , I've seen people on
mobile use picsart to edit , and I've seen people
use paint tool sai 2 and photopea as well ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **for gifs** I use a combination of [pixlr e](https://pixlr.com/e) and
[ezgif](https://ezgif.com) personally , most people also use ezgif
paired with their go-to editing app/website
when they edit gifs ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **fade effects** on images and gifs like [this](https://i.postimg.cc/gj953XX8/1352647j-NPZK5-Qr.gif) can
be done on [online image editor](https://www.online-image-editor.com) ; upload the image
or gif you want on the site , and then click advanced >
viginette ; for visual instructions I've screenshotted
the steps [here](https://postimg.cc/gallery/DJGCpgD/804ac731) ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **recoloring pixels** can be done in two ways ! whenever
I'm making a rentry with a loose color scheme , I go to
[ezgif.com](https://ezgif.com) > effects , and play around with the settings
from there ^^ I've also seen a post about this on tumblr
from [engagekiss](https://www.tumblr.com/engagekiss) ; " che edits pixels on ezgif ( under effects
section ) ; 10 brightness , -10 - -25 contrast , tint whatever
color you want && viola ! " <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **to recolor** pixels in a more specific way , and get them
just how you want them to be , you can go to [ezgif.com](https://ezgif.com) > split !
then , save all the files and recolor each file individually ( time
consuming , I know >:( ) ; after you've done this , name the files
alphabetically or numerically in the order you want each to appear
in your gif , and then go to ezgif.com > gif maker to construct it into
a recolored pixel ! ( if the speed is a bit off , you can go to the [speed](https://ezgif.com/speed)
setting too ) <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **resizing flags** is pretty simple , you can find some on [tumblr](https://tumblr.com) !
after you get one that you want to resize , go to [ezgif](https://ezgif.com) > resize ; make
the height 16 and the width 0 , then upload on an image hosting site
like [postimages](https://postimages.org) ! ( originally from [this](https://www.pinterest.ca/pin/33847434693004792) post on pinterest ) <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **cool animations** can be done with the site [photomosh](https://photomosh.com) , upload
an image and then to go " gif " to add effects ; it's a super helpful
site for making / adding effects onto gifs especially ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **using inspiration** in general is very important ! it's easy to forget ,
as I'm sure that many people feel that they have to be original , but
inspiration is *crucial* for you to improve. especially if you see something
you like , it's a lot better to take inspiration from that , rather than ending
up with something you don't even like for the sake of being original ! <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **similarly** , when using something as inspiration , make sure to
study what you like about it in detail ! if you're not entirely sure what it is
that makes it look so pretty , try to imitate all the aspects of it in a graphic
( ex. the specific thickness of the outline , the colors , the complexity and
the little details ) ^^ going through this kind of process will help you find
a graphic making " style " , which would be compiled of characteristics you
personally find pretty in graphics , that you choose to continue to repeat
or innovate into your next ones ! :D <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **gif over gif** is an overlay feature that [ezgif](https://ezgif.com) does not have as of right now !
instead , you can use [online image editor](https://www.online-image-editor.com) to do this , since it accepts gifs in
the [overlay](https://i.postimg.cc/QCqwJ0VT/image.png) feature ! >:D <-

ã…¤
-> â™¡ **additionally** , [online image editor](https://www.online-image-editor.com) removes the background of " photomoshed "
text and images very well , through its [transparency](https://i.postimg.cc/hPm87fz9/image.png) feature ! though it can be
a hit or miss sometimes , if you keep adjusting the [fuzz](https://i.postimg.cc/25yqCHP8/image.png) options , it could
genuinely save the day LOLDOWAK <-
