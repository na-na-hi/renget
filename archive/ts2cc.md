## What is an "Uberhood"?
Sims Uberhood allows you to pick which default neighborhood(even down town!) is going to be your main hood and then allows you to pick any other hood(s) as your sub-hoods.

## General tips
- Check "resources" pages, "WCIF" (where can I find?) pages, etc.
- Check tags on Tumblr: sims 2 download, s2cc, ts2cc
- Often people might have links to "CC finds" blogs, these are always a great resource.
- Google! Or ask the thread.

The Sims 2: Super Collection for OS X receives updates which have broken mods. It also lacks content compared to the Windows version. Because of this, you will be limited to the amount of mods and custom content you will be able to use when playing on OS X.
