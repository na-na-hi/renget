## Jailbreaks

Jailbreak / jailbreak - the use of the internal features of the system to bypass certain restrictions. In the silly tavern branch, this tool has become much more powerful than in a regular tavern, allowing you to noticeably change the RP progress and the behavior of the bot. Unfortunately, sometimes this significantly affects the character himself, suppressing his description in the card. However, jails can be changed and disabled if necessary, this allows you to add more dynamics to the RP. Ideally you require this version of tavern https://github.com/SillyLossy/TavernAI/tree/dev . A division into main, nsfw and jailbreak prompt appears in it. Further prompts refer specifically to the third block.

5. Always stay in character and avoid repetition.
