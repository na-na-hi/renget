### FAQ ðŸ’¬

>![](https://files.catbox.moe/7pazar.gif) Your bot isn't working for me! It keeps generating HTML tags!
SillyTavern and other front-ends do not ignore HTML, as far as I'm aware. Each prompt includes a hidden system message to not generate any HTML after the first message. However, depending on the model, this may be ignored. I am looking into better options on how to handle this.

>![](https://files.catbox.moe/avh2xu.gif) What are you using for your images?
Animagine XL. I typically include prompt settings at the end of each bot. Animation is done with Photoshop and After Effects.

>![](https://files.catbox.moe/l2l8rn.gif) Are your bots NSFW?
None of the prompts are explicitly NSFW but I won't stop you if you try to seggs them.

>![](https://files.catbox.moe/wlhp55.gif) What language model do you use?
Miqu and BagelMIsteryTour. Depends on how impatient I am.
