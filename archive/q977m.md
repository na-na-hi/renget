It will say and do pretty much anything.
The trick with Claude is to keep it simple and just keep it to the things you want.
Just remove anything you don't want or add some of the things you like best.

If you want to use any other things during a conversation or to change how something is going just use `[System Note: Say something here.]`. Obviously replace the `Say something here` part with what you want :). It may also be useful to tell it `for every message from now on ...`.
